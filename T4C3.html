Clue 4: The Fibonacci Code

Da Vinci often used codes to encode secrets. In this puzzle, the Fibonacci sequence holds a hidden word, with each number corresponding to a letter in the alphabet.

Here are the Fibonacci numbers:

1, 1, 2, 4, 5, 6, 9, 9, 12, 13, 14, 20, 21, 21

Now, convert each Fibonacci number to a letter, where 1 = A, 2 = B, 3 = C, and so on. What hidden word do you get from the first few Fibonacci numbers?

The first letters are: 1 (A), 1 (A), 2 (B), 3 (C), 5 (E), 8 (H), 13 (M), 21 (U), 34 (Invalid), 55 (Invalid)

Note: Ignore the Fibonacci numbers larger than 26 (since the alphabet has only 26 letters).

Hint: The hidden word is something related to Da Vinci's art.

Correct! The hidden word is "A BEAUTIFUL MIND". Let's move on to the next clue!
