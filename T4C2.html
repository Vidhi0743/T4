Clue 2: A riddle to challenge your mind.

This riddle is inspired by Da Vinci's genius and his inventions. Solve it to find the key!

I am not alive, but I grow; I do not have lungs, but I need air; I do not have a mouth, and yet I can drown. What am I?

Hint: Think about something Da Vinci would have studied or designed related to nature.

Correct! The answer is "Fire". Da Vinci was fascinated by the natural elements, and fire is one of them. Now, on to the next clue!
